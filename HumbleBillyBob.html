Stackerdecks

UI/UX | Design | Project Management

A fully realized platform

I designed and built Stackerdecks.com, a complex, responsive piece of tech, from original concept, design artwork and wireframes, to the launch of multiple products across web and mobile. Stackerdecks is a social media platform and cutting-edge slideshow technology. Stackerdecks allows users to collect any social media post, blog post, product page, memes, animated GIF and more into a slideshow, using a simple bookmarklet, or Chrome/Safari extension. Stackerdecks are embeddable across the web and sharable across multiple social media platforms. Built and implemented all UI/UX. Designed all web/print product and marketing collateral. Managed, software engineers, server administrators, and colleagues. Managed projects within specified timelines, and executed multiple project tasks with tight deadlines, while prioritizing competing deadlines and multi-tasks.
